# 🎓 Campus Connect

> **A Full-Stack Platform for Smart Hostel, Mess & Academic Management**

Campus Connect is a full-stack campus utility platform designed to simplify the daily life of college students, especially those living in hostels. The application digitizes essential campus services such as mess management, hostel complaint management, and academic updates while improving communication between students and the administration.

🌐 **Live Demo:** https://smart-campus-management-system-amber.vercel.app/

📂 **GitHub Repository:** https://github.com/Ashis-svg/Smart-Campus-Management-System

---

# 📖 About the Project

Campus Connect was developed to solve the common day-to-day problems faced by hostel students by providing a centralized digital platform for campus services. Instead of relying on manual processes, students can access mess services, submit complaints, track academic updates, and stay informed from a single application.

The project focuses on improving communication between students and the college administration while reducing paperwork and unnecessary delays.

## Problems Solved

### 🍽 Mess Management

The Mess Module helps reduce food wastage by allowing students to submit their attendance separately for breakfast, lunch, and dinner for the following day. Based on the expected attendance, the mess management can estimate the quantity of food required.

Students can also:

* View today's menu
* View tomorrow's menu
* Submit meal attendance
* Rate the food quality
* Provide daily feedback
* View mess reports and ratings
* Read important mess notices

---

### 🏠 Hostel Complaint Management

Students can submit complaints directly through the application without visiting the hostel office.

Room-specific complaints such as:

* Furniture issues
* Electrical issues
* General room maintenance

are automatically linked to the student's hostel and room details, allowing the administration to identify the complaint location immediately.

The application also includes a **General Complaint** section where students can report common issues such as:

* Water cooler not working
* Corridor lights
* Washroom maintenance
* Cleanliness

Other students can support these complaints by voting, helping the administration identify high-priority issues more efficiently.

---

### 📚 Academics

The Academics module helps students stay updated with their academic schedule.

Students can:

* View today's classes
* View tomorrow's classes
* Track attendance percentage

This provides a single place to monitor daily academic activities.

# 💡 Motivation

The primary goal of Campus Connect is to create a centralized platform that improves campus life by making hostel and mess management more transparent, organized, and efficient.

Instead of visiting different offices or relying on notice boards, students can complete most of their daily campus-related activities from one application.
